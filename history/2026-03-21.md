# Today in History - March 21

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1946  
📝 Event: The Los Angeles Rams sign Kenny Washington, making him the first African American player in professional American football since 1933.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1965  
📝 Event: Martin Luther King Jr. leads 3,200 people on the start of the third and finally successful civil rights march from Selma to Montgomery, Alabama.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1925  
📝 Event: The Butler Act prohibits the teaching of human evolution in Tennessee.
