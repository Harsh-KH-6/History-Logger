# Today in History - April 15

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1715  
📝 Event: The Pocotaligo Massacre triggers the start of the Yamasee War in colonial South Carolina.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1861  
📝 Event: President Abraham Lincoln calls for 75,000 militiamen to quell the insurrection that soon became the American Civil War.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1817  
📝 Event: Thomas Hopkins Gallaudet and Laurent Clerc found the American School for the Deaf (then called the Connecticut Asylum for the Education and Instruction of Deaf and Dumb Persons), the first American school for deaf students, in Hartford, Connecticut.
