# Today in History - April 22

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1836  
📝 Event: Texas Revolution: A day after the Battle of San Jacinto, forces under Texas General Sam Houston identify Mexican General Antonio López de Santa Anna among the captives of the battle when some of his fellow soldiers mistakenly give away his identity.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1876  
📝 Event: The first National League baseball game is played at the Jefferson Street Grounds in Philadelphia.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1974  
📝 Event: Pan Am Flight 812 crashes on approach to Ngurah Rai International Airport in Denpasar, Bali, Indonesia, killing all 107 people on board.
