# Today in History - July 12

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1812  
📝 Event: The American Army of the Northwest briefly occupies the Upper Canadian settlement at what is now at Windsor, Ontario.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 2007  
📝 Event: U.S. Army Apache helicopters engage in airstrikes against armed insurgents in Baghdad, Iraq, where civilians are killed; footage from the cockpit is later leaked to the Internet.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1918  
📝 Event: The Imperial Japanese Navy battleship Kawachi blows up at Shunan, western Honshu, Japan, killing at least 621.
