# Today in History - March 25

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1959  
📝 Event: Chain Island is sold by the State of California to Russell Gallaway III, a Sacramento businessman who plans to use it as a "hunting and fishing retreat", for $5,258.20 ($58,072 in 2025).

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1655  
📝 Event: Saturn's largest moon, Titan, is discovered by Christiaan Huygens.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 919  
📝 Event: Romanos Lekapenos seizes the Boukoleon Palace in Constantinople and becomes regent of the Byzantine emperor Constantine VII.
