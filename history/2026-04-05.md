# Today in History - April 05

## Event 1
📅 Year: 919  
📝 Event: The second Fatimid invasion of Egypt begins, when the Fatimid heir-apparent, al-Qa'im bi-Amr Allah, sets out from Raqqada at the head of his army.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 823  
📝 Event: Lothair I is crowned King of Italy by Pope Paschal I.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1566  
📝 Event: Two hundred Dutch noblemen, led by Hendrick van Brederode, force themselves into the presence of Margaret of Parma and present the Petition of Compromise, denouncing the Spanish Inquisition in the Seventeen Provinces.
