# Today in History - April 06

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1994  
📝 Event: The Rwandan genocide begins when the aircraft carrying Rwandan president Juvénal Habyarimana and Burundian president Cyprien Ntaryamira is shot down.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1652  
📝 Event: At the Cape of Good Hope, Dutch sailor Jan van Riebeeck establishes a resupply camp that eventually becomes Cape Town.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1957  
📝 Event: The flag carrier airline of Greece for decades, Olympic Airways, is founded by Aristotle Onassis following the acquisition of "TAE - Greek National Airlines".
