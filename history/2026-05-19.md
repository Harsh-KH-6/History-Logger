# Today in History - May 19

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1535  
📝 Event: French explorer Jacques Cartier sets sail on his second voyage to North America with three ships, 110 men, and Chief Donnacona's two sons (whom Cartier had kidnapped during his first voyage).

## Event 2

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1780  
📝 Event: New England's Dark Day, an unusual darkening of the day sky, was observed over the New England states and parts of Canada.
