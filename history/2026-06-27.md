# Today in History - June 27

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1973  
📝 Event: The President of Uruguay Juan María Bordaberry dissolves Parliament and establishes a dictatorship.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1954  
📝 Event: The Obninsk Nuclear Power Plant, the Soviet Union's first nuclear power station, opens in Obninsk, near Moscow.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1981  
📝 Event: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party issues its "Resolution on Certain Questions in the History of Our Party Since the Founding of the People's Republic of China", laying the blame for the Cultural Revolution on Mao Zedong.
