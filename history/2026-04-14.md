# Today in History - April 14

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1865  
📝 Event: U.S. President Abraham Lincoln is shot in Ford's Theatre by John Wilkes Booth; Lincoln dies the following day.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1929  
📝 Event: The inaugural Monaco Grand Prix takes place in the Principality of Monaco. William Grover-Williams wins driving a Bugatti Type 35.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1945  
📝 Event: Razing of Friesoythe: The 4th Canadian (Armoured) Division deliberately destroys the German town of Friesoythe on the orders of Major General Christopher Vokes.
