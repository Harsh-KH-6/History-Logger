# Today in History - June 21

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1982  
📝 Event: John Hinckley is found not guilty by reason of insanity for the attempted assassination of U.S. President Ronald Reagan.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1993  
📝 Event: Space Shuttle Endeavour is launched on STS-57 to retrieve the European Retrievable Carrier (EURECA) satellite. It is also the first shuttle mission to carry the Spacehab module.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1919  
📝 Event: Admiral Ludwig von Reuter scuttles the German fleet at Scapa Flow, Orkney. The nine sailors killed are the last casualties of World War I.
