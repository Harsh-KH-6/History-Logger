# Today in History - June 18

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1429  
📝 Event: Charles VII's army defeats an English army under John Talbot at the Battle of Patay during the Hundred Years' War. The English lost 2,200 men, over half their army, crippling their efforts during this segment of the war.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1812  
📝 Event: The United States declaration of war upon the United Kingdom is signed by President James Madison, beginning the War of 1812.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1940  
📝 Event: Appeal of 18 June by Charles de Gaulle.
