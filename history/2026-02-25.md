# Today in History - February 25

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1918  
📝 Event: World War I: German forces capture Tallinn to virtually complete the occupation of Estonia.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1932  
📝 Event: Adolf Hitler, having been stateless for seven years, obtains German citizenship when he is appointed a Brunswick state official by Dietrich Klagges, a fellow Nazi. As a result, Hitler is able to run for Reichspräsident in the 1932 election.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1836  
📝 Event: Samuel Colt is granted a United States patent for his revolver firearm.
