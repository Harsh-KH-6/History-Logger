# Today in History - April 01

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1945  
📝 Event: World War II: The Tenth United States Army attacks the Thirty-Second Japanese Army on Okinawa.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1949  
📝 Event: Chinese Civil War: The Chinese Communist Party holds unsuccessful peace talks with the Nationalist Party in Beijing, after three years of fighting.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1081  
📝 Event: Alexios I Komnenos overthrows the Byzantine emperor Nikephoros III Botaneiates, and, after his troops spend three days extensively looting Constantinople, is formally crowned on April 4.
