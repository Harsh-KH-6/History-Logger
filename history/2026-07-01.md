# Today in History - July 01

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1643  
📝 Event: First meeting of the Westminster Assembly, a council of theologians ("divines") and members of the Parliament of England appointed to restructure the Church of England, at Westminster Abbey in London.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1999  
📝 Event: The Scottish Parliament is officially opened by Elizabeth II on the day that legislative powers are officially transferred from the old Scottish Office in London to the new devolved Scottish Executive in Edinburgh. In Wales, the powers of the Welsh Secretary are transferred to the National Assembly.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1968  
📝 Event: Formal separation of the United Auto Workers from the AFL-CIO in the United States.
