# Today in History - July 24

## Event 1
📅 Year: 1534  
📝 Event: French explorer Jacques Cartier plants a cross on the Gaspé Peninsula and takes possession of the territory in the name of Francis I of France.

## Event 2
📅 Year: 1966  
📝 Event: Michael Pelkey makes the first BASE jump from El Capitan along with Brian Schubert. Both came out with broken bones. BASE jumping has now been banned from El Cap.

## Event 3
📅 Year: 1998  
📝 Event: Russell Eugene Weston Jr. bursts into the United States Capitol and opens fire killing two police officers. He is later ruled to be incompetent to stand trial.
